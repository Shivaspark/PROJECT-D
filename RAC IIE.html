Who We Are

The Rotaract Club of Info Institute of Engineering is a formidable clan of students and young professionals. We are part of a global network of Rotaract clubs, united under the banner of Rotary International.

Our quest is to empower young men and women, honing the skills needed for personal growth, to conquer the challenges facing our communities, and to forge unbreakable bonds of friendship and service across the world.

The Rotaract Codex

Founded on the pillars of Service Above Self, we stand as guardians of change in our realm. Our code is simple yet profound: to serve with honor, lead with wisdom, and inspire with action. We believe that every young leader has the power to forge a legacy that transcends time.

Through community service, professional development, international understanding, and leadership training, we shape not just careers, but characters. Our fellowship extends beyond borders, creating a global network of change-makers who share a common vision of a better world.

Our Flagships Projects 2025 - 2026

Forging a lasting legacy in our realm.

DAYA 2026

Daya is a legacy project of the Rotaract Club of INFO Institute of Engineering. Through this initiative, we have brought together 500+ orphaned children, engaging them in games and cultural activities. This project is not just a celebration, but a day dedicated to caring for and honoring these children. The true essence of Daya lies in its theme — Power of Mercy.

ROBO 2026

ROBO (Rise of Bright Opportunities) is a project of the Rotaract Club of INFO Institute of Engineering aimed at creating career opportunities for students and Rotaractors.
